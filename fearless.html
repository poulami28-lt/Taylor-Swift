Fearless

Taylor Swift released her second album, Fearless, in November 2008. It topped the Billboard 200 for 11 weeks and was the best-selling U.S. album of 2009. Hits like “Love Story” and “You Belong with Me” broke records on pop and country charts. She launched her first headlining Fearless Tour and won multiple awards, including Album of the Year at the Grammys and Entertainer of the Year at the CMAs. In 2009, she also collaborated with artists like John Mayer and Boys Like Girls, contributed to movie soundtracks, and made her acting debut in Valentine’s Day. That year, she also hosted and performed on Saturday Night Live, becoming the first host to write their own monologue.
